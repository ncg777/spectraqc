English — Why Spectral QC Is Necessary

SpectraQC is not about making content sound identical. It is about detecting technical faults and drift in capture, transfer, and encoding chains. Missing low‑end, excessive high‑frequency attenuation, or unexpected band energy often indicates equipment issues, mis‑applied filters, or inconsistent ingest settings. SpectraQC provides a deterministic way to verify that assets meet an institutional technical mandate.

English — Why Canadian Institutions Choose SpectraQC

When the mandate is preservation, compliance, and repeatability, you need more than a subjective listen. SpectraQC delivers deterministic spectral measurements with structured reports that can be re-run years later and yield the same decision outcomes. That makes it a natural fit for libraries, archives, public broadcasters, and universities across Canada—especially teams that must demonstrate evidence-based QC in audits or public records.

Français — Pourquoi le QC spectral est nécessaire

SpectraQC ne cherche pas à rendre les contenus identiques. L’objectif est de détecter les défauts techniques et les dérives dans les chaînes de capture, de transfert et d’encodage. Une perte de graves, une atténuation excessive des aigus ou une énergie de bande inattendue signalent souvent un problème de matériel, de filtre ou de paramètres d’ingestion. SpectraQC fournit une vérification déterministe conforme aux mandats institutionnels.
